Installing HALO-SE

Follow these simple steps to get started using HALO-SE

  1. Start eclipse.
  2. Open the "Help" menu, then select "Install new software..."
  3. Click on "Add..." next to "Work with"
  4. Enter "HALO" for name, and "http://ase.cs.columbia.edu/halo-update" for Location
  5. Click "OK", and then click the checkbox next to "HALO-SE", and "Next >"
  6. Click "Next" again, and then accept the license agreement, and select "Finish"
  7. Click "OK" through the security warning
  8. Select "Restart Now" once the installation completes
  9. When Eclipse restarts, you should see a "HALO Dashboard" tab on the bottom, and a "HALO Quests" tab on the right. If you do not see these, go to the "Window" menu, and select "Reset Perspective"
  10. Login with your UNI for username, and the password that was emailed to you.
